7. Interpretation

State only what the evidence supports. Allowed wording includes visibility observed, traffic observed, downloads observed, DOI events observed, external reference observed, citation observed, and practical use observed. Avoid adoption, recognition, acceptance, institutional use, research citation, or validation claims unless directly evidenced.
